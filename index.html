What is a Jazious Carmankle?
  • Jazious Carmankles were first discovered by San Juan de Pantalones in the year 1592, as part of his expedition to a previously unexplored region of the new world. He first described them as a cross between breadfruit and a cravat, though later said they most resembled his ex-wife. The name Jazious Carmankle was given to this strange item by the brave explorer because his ship's name was Jazious Carmankle and he wasn't a very creative person. Why his ship was named that is a story for another time and place.
